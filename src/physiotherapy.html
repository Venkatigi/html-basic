Saveetha College of Physiotherapy

Saveetha College of Physiotherapy is dedicated to providing high-quality education and training in physiotherapy. Located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, the college offers both undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

The faculty at Saveetha College of Physiotherapy consists of experienced professionals who focus on practical learning and research, preparing students for successful careers in healthcare and rehabilitation.
