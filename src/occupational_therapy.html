Saveetha College of Occupational Therapy

Saveetha College of Occupational Therapy offers specialized undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Occupational Therapy. Located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, the college focuses on holistic healthcare approaches and practical skills development.

Students at Saveetha College of Occupational Therapy benefit from hands-on training, clinical experience, and academic rigor. The college prepares graduates to provide effective therapeutic interventions and promote health and wellness in diverse populations.
