Saveetha School of Hospitality and Tourism

Saveetha School of Hospitality and Tourism offers comprehensive programs that prepare students for careers in the vibrant hospitality industry. Located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, the school provides hands-on training, industry exposure, and academic excellence. Students at Saveetha School of Hospitality and Tourism gain practical skills in hotel management, tourism operations, culinary arts, event management, and more. The school focuses on nurturing hospitality professionals with global perspectives and leadership qualities.
