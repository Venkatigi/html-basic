Saveetha Pupil Eco School

Saveetha Pupil Eco School is dedicated to providing a sustainable and eco-friendly educational environment for children. Located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, the school integrates environmental education with academic curriculum to foster holistic development.

Students at Saveetha Pupil Eco School engage in activities that promote environmental awareness, conservation practices, and hands-on learning experiences. The school aims to nurture responsible global citizens who are committed to environmental stewardship.
